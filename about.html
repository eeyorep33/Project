The Debugging joins us from Amsterdam. This band has a soulful sound and a colourful style, bringing reckless guitars, hairy rhythm, fast vocals, and arbitrary songwriting to the table. The band has been cooking legs since 2017. The Debugging plays tunes from a variety of genres, and their influences include bands like Evanescence and RHCP. Look out for The Debugging coming to your local tennis court this spring.

Cicada: Saxaphone

Cicada has been playing the saxaphone since he was 2 years old, much to the horror of his neighbors.Producing ear screeching sounds, Cicada plays the saxaphone as often and as loudly as possible.

Spider: Drums

Spider may have only been playing the drums for 2 months, but in that 2 months he has figured out how to not hit himself in the head with the drumsticks! Great job, Spider!

Mosquito: Bass Guitar

Mosquito has been jamming on the bass guitar in his parents basement(where he still lives) for many years. He claims the acoustics in the basement are just so good he sees no reason to move out.

Wasp: Banjo

Wasp is a little bit county and a little bit rock and roll with his whimsical banjo stylings. It doesn't really sound good with the rest of the band, but we felt bad saying no.

Daddy Long Legs: Lead Vocals

Affectionately referred to as "Daddy", Mr. Long Legs screams and wails his heart out on and off the stage. In fact he can't seem to help himself; which is rather embarrassing in the grocery store.

Mantis: Keyboard

Mantis actually goes on stage with her dog. The little porker waddles around stage and wags in sync to the music. We're also pretty sure the dog can play the keyboard better than Mantis.

Locust: Lead Guitar

Locust loves playing his guitar so much he calls it "Precious"(you need to say it in the creepy voice to get the full effect). He sleeps with it, goes to the store with it and cuddles with it. Too bad he doesn't practice with it.
